About Us

Crestwood Academy is committed to providing a nurturing environment where students can excel academically, socially, and personally. Our personalized learning plans, state-of-the-art facilities, and focus on holistic development ensure that each student reaches their full potential.

Our Mission

At Crestwood Academy, our mission is to foster a lifelong love of learning in our students, empowering them to become responsible, respectful, and resourceful members of society. We strive to provide an inclusive and supportive environment that nurtures the intellectual, social, and emotional growth of each student.

Our History

Established in 1985, Crestwood Academy has a rich history of academic excellence and innovation. Over the past three decades, we have grown from a small local school to a well-respected institution known for our rigorous academic programs, dedicated faculty, and vibrant school community.

Our Values

  • Integrity: We believe in honesty, fairness, and respect in all our interactions.
  • Excellence: We strive for the highest standards in academics, extracurricular activities, and personal conduct.
  • Community: We foster a sense of belonging and collaboration among students, staff, and parents.
  • Innovation: We encourage creativity and critical thinking to solve problems and embrace new ideas.
  • Respect: We celebrate diversity and promote a culture of inclusion and mutual respect.

Unique Features

Crestwood Academy stands out for its unique approach to education, which includes:

  • Personalized Learning Plans: Tailored education plans to meet the individual needs of each student.
  • State-of-the-Art Facilities: Modern classrooms, science labs, sports facilities, and arts studios.
  • Holistic Development: A focus on academic, physical, and emotional well-being.
  • Extracurricular Activities: A wide range of clubs, sports teams, and arts programs to foster student interests and talents.
  • Community Engagement: Opportunities for students to engage with and give back to the community through service projects and partnerships.
