Academics

At Crestwood Academy, we offer a comprehensive and rigorous academic program designed to challenge and inspire students. Our curriculum is crafted to foster critical thinking, creativity, and a love of learning in every student.

Curriculum Overview

Our curriculum is designed to provide a balanced education, integrating core academic subjects with the arts, physical education, and technology. We emphasize hands-on learning experiences and encourage students to explore their interests and passions.

Teaching Methods

We employ a variety of teaching methods to cater to the diverse learning styles of our students. Our educators use a mix of traditional instruction, collaborative projects, and technology-enhanced learning to create an engaging and dynamic classroom environment.

Student Support Services

We offer a range of support services to ensure that all students have the resources they need to succeed. Our support services include academic counseling, tutoring, special education programs, and mental health services. We are committed to providing a supportive and inclusive learning environment for every student.

Extracurricular Activities

We believe that education extends beyond the classroom. Our school offers a wide range of extracurricular activities, including sports teams, music and arts programs, clubs, and community service opportunities. These activities help students develop leadership skills, teamwork, and a sense of community.
