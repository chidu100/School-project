Upcoming Events

Join us for our upcoming events. Stay engaged and be a part of our vibrant community.

Spring Concert

Join us for our annual Spring Concert on May 30th at 6 PM in the school auditorium. Our talented students will showcase their musical skills, featuring performances from the school choir, orchestra, and band. This is a wonderful opportunity to enjoy an evening of music and celebrate the hard work of our students. All are welcome to attend, and admission is free.

Science Fair

The Crestwood Academy Science Fair will be held on June 10th from 9 AM to 3 PM in the school gymnasium. Students from all grade levels will present their science projects and experiments. This event encourages students to explore their scientific interests and develop their research skills. Awards will be given for the best projects in various categories. Parents, teachers, and community members are invited to attend and support our young scientists.

Sports Day

Sports Day is scheduled for June 25th from 8 AM to 2 PM on the school grounds. This event is a celebration of physical fitness and team spirit. Students will participate in a variety of athletic events, including track and field, soccer, basketball, and more. There will be competitions, games, and fun activities for everyone. Parents are encouraged to attend and cheer on their children. Refreshments will be available throughout the day.

Back to School Night

Our annual Back to School Night will take place on September 15th at 6 PM. This event is an opportunity for parents and guardians to meet their children's teachers, learn about the curriculum, and explore the school facilities. There will be presentations in the classrooms, and teachers will be available to answer any questions. We look forward to welcoming our families and building a strong partnership for the school year ahead.

Art Exhibition

The Crestwood Academy Art Exhibition will be held on October 20th from 10 AM to 4 PM in the school art gallery. Our talented art students will display their paintings, sculptures, and digital art creations. This exhibition is a celebration of creativity and artistic expression. Visitors will have the opportunity to view and purchase student artwork. Light refreshments will be served.

Winter Carnival

Join us for the annual Winter Carnival on December 10th from 11 AM to 5 PM. The event will feature carnival games, food stalls, a talent show, and a visit from Santa Claus. This is a fun-filled day for the whole family. All proceeds from the carnival will go towards school programs and activities. Don't miss out on this exciting event!

Book Fair

The Book Fair will be held from January 15th to January 20th in the school library. Students, parents, and teachers are invited to browse and purchase books from a wide selection of genres and age groups. The Book Fair is a great opportunity to encourage reading and support our school library. Special events, including author readings and book signings, will be held throughout the week.

Drama Club Performance

The Drama Club will present their annual performance on February 25th at 7 PM in the school theater. This year's production is a classic play, brought to life by our talented student actors and actresses. Tickets are available for purchase in advance or at the door. Come and enjoy a night of theater and support our drama students.
